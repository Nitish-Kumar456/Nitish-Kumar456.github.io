FAQ related to mobiles

  • What type of battery has the longest life?

What does "Dual Band Phone" mean?

An analog and digital service can both be used on a dual band phone.

Which battery kind has the longest lifespan?

The longest-lasting batteries are lithium-ion or Li-ion batteries. Additionally, they are the lightest in weight. The price is the negative indicated by the two pluses. The most expensive battery type will always be a li-ion battery.

What's the difference between the Stand-by time and Talk time?

Your phone is in standby mode when your phone is on but you aren't talking. The phone's power consumption is low, and it can remain in this mode for an extended period of time. You use talk time when you're using the phone. At a much faster rate, it consumes energy from the battery. Somewhere between talking time and standby time, your battery life will be.
